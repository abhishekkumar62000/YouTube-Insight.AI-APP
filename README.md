### 💡 **Why I Built This?**  
I often found myself watching **long videos** but struggling to take **proper notes** or extract **key insights efficiently**. So, I built **YouTube Insight.AI** to automate this process using **AI**.  

This tool is for anyone who wants to **learn faster, retain better, and get AI-powered insights** from YouTube videos.

## 🎯 **Key Features (10 Advanced AI Capabilities)**  

### 🎯 **Current App Features**  
✅ **🏠 Home** – Navigate easily through the app  
✅ **📝 Get Detailed Notes** – Generate AI-powered notes for any video  
✅ **🌍 Translate Summary** – Translate summaries into multiple languages  
✅ **🎧 AI Voice Summary** – Listen to AI-generated audio summaries  
✅ **📄 Download Summary** – Save video summaries for offline use  
✅ **📊 Analyze Sentiment** – Get sentiment analysis of video transcripts  
✅ **🔍 Search in Transcript** – Quickly find keywords within transcripts  
✅ **📤 Export Chapters as JSON** – Export structured chapter data  
✅ **🌙 Dark Mode** – Enjoy a sleek dark theme  
✅ **🌈 Theme Selection** – Customize the app's appearance  
✅ **🌍 Detect Transcript Language** – Automatically detect the transcript language

- ✅ Streamlit 🌐
